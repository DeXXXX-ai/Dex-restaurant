Our Chefs

Gordon Ramsay, a world-renowned chef and restaurateur, is famous for his fiery passion for cooking and uncompromising standards. Known for his Michelin-starred restaurants and TV shows, Ramsay has a deep appreciation for crafting the perfect burger. His burger journey gained attention with the opening of Gordon Ramsay Burger in Las Vegas, where he redefined the classic dish using premium ingredients like Wagyu beef and brioche buns. Ramsay decided to join Dex Restaurants to bring his expertise to a global platform, aligning with their mission to elevate burgers to an art form and reach food lovers worldwide. Together, they aim to redefine gourmet burgers with innovative techniques and exceptional quality.
